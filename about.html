About CardCraft

The free, private way to design a professional business card.

Home › About

CardCraft is a free, browser-based business card maker built for people who want a clean, professional card without wrestling with complicated design software. Whether you are a freelancer, a small business owner, a student, or someone preparing for a networking event, CardCraft helps you produce a polished card in just a few minutes.

Our mission

We believe good design should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford a designer or expensive software subscriptions. Our mission is simple: make it effortless to create a business card that looks like it cost money to design — for free, and without compromising your privacy.

Our story

CardCraft started as a small side project to solve a common frustration. Many online card tools hide essential features behind paywalls, require you to create an account before you can even try them, or upload your personal information to remote servers. We wanted something different: a tool that works instantly, keeps your data on your own device, and never asks for a credit card.

Over time the project grew into the focused tool you see today — a handful of carefully designed templates, a built-in image editor, custom colour options, and high-quality PDF and PNG exports.

What makes us different

  • Truly free. Every feature is available to everyone, with no hidden upsells.
  • Privacy first. All processing happens in your browser. We do not store your photos or details on any server.
  • Print quality. Exports use the standard card size with crisp vector text that prints sharp.
  • No friction. No sign-up, no downloads, no waiting. Just open the page and start designing.

Who we are for

CardCraft is built for freelancers, consultants, shop owners, event hosts, real-estate agents, content creators, job seekers, and anyone who wants a memorable card to hand out. If you have ever wished you could make a quick, attractive card without the fuss, this tool is for you.

Get in touch

We are always improving CardCraft and we love hearing from the people who use it. If you have a suggestion, found a bug, or just want to say hello, head over to our contact page. To start designing right now, visit the card builder.
